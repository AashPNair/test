About

My Background

Exploring the diverse cultural arts and cuisines of different locales has always been an inspirational journey for me.
Growing up in various parts of India, my childhood memories are filled with foods and flavors from so many different places.
The desire for discovering newer international culinary recipes accentuated after I moved to the United States a decade ago.
With this passion for food and cooking, my Channel ‘Soul Curries by Sneha Nair', is a humble effort to
showcase the unique and traditional flavors and recipes from the East and the West, to warm your Heart and Soul...❤️
